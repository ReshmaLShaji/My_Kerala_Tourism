Welcome to Thekkady Tourism - Gateway to Wilderness

Step into the captivating world of Thekkady, the gateway to wilderness and an adventurer's paradise. Thekkady Tourism invites you to explore the pristine beauty of this nature-rich destination and experience the thrill of its wildlife encounters.


Embrace the Wilderness of Periyar National Park

At the heart of Thekkady lies the renowned Periyar National Park, a haven for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. Embark on an exhilarating jungle safari and get a chance to spot elephants, tigers, wild boars, and an array of bird species amidst the lush greenery.


Boat Safari on Periyar Lake - A Mesmerizing Experience

Sail through the calm waters of Periyar Lake on a boat safari, surrounded by the misty hills and dense forests. Witness the animals as they come to the lake for a drink, creating unforgettable moments in the lap of nature.


Nature Treks - Immerse in the Serenity

Explore the various nature trails in Thekkady, where every step unveils the beauty of the Western Ghats. Trek to the vantage points, breathe in the fresh air, and soak in the breathtaking panoramas of the surrounding landscapes.


Spice Plantations - A Fragrant Experience

Discover the aromatic world of spice plantations in Thekkady. Walk through the spice-scented pathways, learn about various spices like cardamom, pepper, cinnamon, and cloves, and witness the cultivation process.


Elephant Rides - A Unique Encounter

Interact with the gentle giants of Thekkady on an elephant ride. Enjoy a leisurely journey through the verdant forests, accompanied by the rhythmic footsteps of these magnificent creatures.


Explore Tribal Culture

Get a glimpse into the vibrant tribal culture of Thekkady. Visit tribal villages, interact with the locals, and experience their age-old traditions and art forms.
