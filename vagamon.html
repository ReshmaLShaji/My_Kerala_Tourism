Welcome to Vagamon Tourism - Nature's Tranquil Retreat

Step into the serene world of Vagamon, a hidden gem nestled amidst the Western Ghats. Vagamon Tourism invites you to discover the untouched beauty of this pristine hill station and immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature.


Embrace the Beauty of Vagamon's Hills

Vagamon's picturesque hills, adorned with lush green meadows and pine forests, offer a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The cool climate and panoramic vistas create a soothing ambiance for relaxation and rejuvenation.


Misty Valleys and Dazzling Meadows

Explore the mist-covered valleys and rolling meadows of Vagamon, where every turn reveals a new breathtaking view. The enchanting beauty of the landscape will leave you mesmerized.


Vagamon Pine Forest - A Walk Amidst Serenity

Stroll through the Vagamon Pine Forest, a place of tranquility where the fragrance of pine fills the air. The whispering trees and gentle breeze create a calming atmosphere, perfect for introspection and soulful contemplation.


Vagamon Lake - A Delightful Picnic Spot

Spend quality time by the Vagamon Lake, where the calm waters reflect the surrounding hills. Enjoy a peaceful picnic or indulge in a pedal boat ride, soaking in the idyllic surroundings.


Thrilling Adventure Activities

For the adventurous souls, Vagamon offers a host of exciting activities. Go paragliding and experience the thrill of soaring above the hills, or trek to the famous Thangal Para and Murugan Mala for an adrenaline-pumping adventure.


Meadows of Flowers - Kurisumala

Visit Kurisumala, known for its vast meadows adorned with colorful flowers. The hilltop offers a panoramic view of Vagamon's beauty, making it a photographer's delight.
