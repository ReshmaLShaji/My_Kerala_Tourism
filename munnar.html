Welcome to Munnar Tourism - Where Nature Paints its Masterpiece

Step into the breathtaking world of Munnar, a paradise nestled amidst the Western Ghats. Munnar Tourism invites you to explore the mesmerizing beauty of this hill station, where lush greenery, misty hills, and aromatic tea gardens create a picturesque landscape.


Misty Hills and Tea Plantations

Be captivated by the misty hills and rolling tea gardens that adorn the landscape of Munnar. Wander through the emerald tea plantations, breathe in the refreshing air, and witness the process of tea-making at the tea factories.


Eravikulam National Park - A Sanctuary of Nature

Discover the wonders of Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and an array of flora and fauna. The Anamudi Peak, South India's highest peak, adds to the allure of this sanctuary.


Echo Point - Where Nature Whispers

Echo Point and experience nature's echo phenomenon. Shout out your heart's desire, and hear your voice reverberate through the valleys, creating a magical moment in the lap of nature.


Mattupetty Dam - A Scenic Reservoir

Admire the picturesque Mattupetty Dam, surrounded by rolling hills and tea gardens. Take a boat ride on the tranquil waters and enjoy the serene beauty of the reservoir.


Pothamedu Viewpoint - Panoramic Vistas

Trek to the Pothamedu Viewpoint and witness the panoramic vistas of Munnar's beauty. The view of the mist-clad mountains and the lush valleys below is simply breathtaking.


Adventure Activities - Thrills Unleashed

For adventure enthusiasts, Munnar offers trekking, mountain biking, and rock climbing opportunities. Embark on thrilling journeys through the rugged terrain and feel the adrenaline rush.


Plan Your Munnar Expedition

Munnar Tourism is here to curate your dream escape to nature's canvas. Whether you seek tranquility amidst the hills or an adventure through the wilderness, we have the perfect itinerary for you. Experience the surreal beauty of Munnar, where every moment is a masterpiece of nature's creation. Book your journey now and be enchanted by the allure of this hill station paradise
