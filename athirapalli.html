Welcome to Athirapally Waterfalls - Nature's Majestic Cascade

Enter the realm of awe-inspiring beauty at Athirapally Waterfalls, where nature's grandeur unfolds in all its splendor. Athirapally Waterfalls invites you to witness the majestic cascade and immerse yourself in the serenity of this natural wonder.


The Majestic Athirapally Waterfalls

Marvel at the sight of Athirapally Waterfalls, a grand spectacle where the Chalakudy River cascades down from a height of 80 feet. The thundering roar of the waterfall and the misty spray create a captivating ambiance.


Dense Greenery - A Nature Lover's Paradise

Surrounded by lush greenery and dense forests, Athirapally offers a haven for nature enthusiasts. Embrace the tranquil atmosphere as you wander through the verdant landscape.


Vazhachal Waterfalls - A Twin Marvel

Just a short distance away, the Vazhachal Waterfalls complements the beauty of Athirapally. The gushing waters amidst the green foliage make it a picturesque spot.


Kauthuka Park - Where Nature and Art Unite

Visit Kauthuka Park, a unique garden that blends nature with artistic creations. Admire the sculptures and artwork amidst the natural setting, making it a delightful place to explore.


Thumboormuzhi Dam - A Scenic Retreat

Explore the Thumboormuzhi Dam, where the Chalakudy River flows peacefully, creating a picturesque retreat for visitors. Enjoy the serene atmosphere and panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.


Nature Walks and Treks - Enchanting Trails

Engage in nature walks and treks around Athirapally to immerse yourself in the beauty of the Western Ghats. The trails offer a chance to spot wildlife and enjoy the serene surroundings.
